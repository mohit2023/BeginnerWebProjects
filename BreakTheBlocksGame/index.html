Break The Blocks!

How to play:

Use right and left keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball and break the blocks

If you miss the ball, your score and the blocks will reset.

How to use left and right butttons:

Clicking on right button starts moving it towards right.

Clicking on it again will stop it.

While moving if left button is pressed than it starts towards left.

Similar opeartion works when you click left button first.
